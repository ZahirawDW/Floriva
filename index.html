About Us

Founded in 2023, Floriva began as a passion project between two friends who believed that everyone deserves access to beautiful, fresh flowers. What started as a small neighborhood shop has blossomed into a beloved local florist known for our attention to detail and personalized service. At Floriva, we believe flowers have the power to brighten any day and express emotions when words fall short. Our skilled florists carefully hand-select each stem from local growers and sustainable farms, ensuring only the freshest, most vibrant blooms make it into your arrangements. We take pride in creating custom bouquets that tell your unique story—whether it's celebrating love, offering comfort, or simply bringing a smile to someone's face. Every arrangement is crafted with care, artistry, and a commitment to quality that you can see and feel. Thank you for letting us be part of your special moments and everyday joys.
